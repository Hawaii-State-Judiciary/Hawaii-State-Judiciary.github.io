Analysis 1: Mediation Agreement vs No Mediation Agreement

Rationale: There could be interesting differences in the cases that reach an agreement at mediation vs the ones that do not. For example, it is possible that the cases where an agreement is reached are less “severe” or dire in terms of factors such as the Total Amount Owed or the number of months the tenant is behind on payment. If tenants have higher incomes, that could represent a higher ability to pay, which could also affect the likelihood that a mediation agreement can be reached.

Predictions:

  • Total Amount Owed and Months Behind should be smaller for the cases that reach an agreement at mediation vs no agreement.
  • Higher monthly incomes should be larger for cases that reach an agreement vs not.
